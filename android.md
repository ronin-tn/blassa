1. Splash Screen
Prompt:

Design a Splash Screen for the Blassa Android app.

Background: Gradient from Primary Teal (#0E7490) to a darker teal (#164E63).
Center: The Blassa logo (large, white) with a subtle scale-in animation.
Bottom: A small loading indicator (circular progress in white or amber).
Style: Clean, minimal. The logo should feel like it's emerging from the "sea."
